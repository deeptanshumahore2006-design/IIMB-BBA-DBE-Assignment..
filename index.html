About Me

My Roots

My village, Anjangaon Surji, runs deep in a town defined by grit. We carry a legacy from the Anglo-Maratha Wars—a history of resilience and a warrior spirit.

But we are also a land of abundance. Our rich black soil makes us a hub for Oranges and the rare Long Pepper. Living at the doorstep of the Melghat Tiger Reserve, I carry the essence of a land where history, nature, and hard work meet.
